About us

Euro Hotel Hammersmith is a newly renovated hotel that is modern yet intimate. It is a great place to relax and unwind. The hotel is surrounded by some of London’s best restaurants and bars, trendy cafes and most exciting theatre and nightlife. The Hotel is conveniently located in the heart of West London, moments away from Hammersmith, Shepherds Bush and Kensington Olympia. It is perfect for exploring London, doing retail therapy, attending events and many other attractions that this great city has to offer. Euro Hotel Hammersmith has 44 tastefully decorated retro bedrooms, that will feel like your home away from home. All rooms are comfortable, guaranteeing you a great night’s sleep, especially after so much to explore in London. You will need it.
