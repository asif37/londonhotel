Cancellation Policy

The General Booking can be cancelled up to 48 hours or 2 days before the arrival and no fee will be charged. However, if it is cancelled later or in case of no-show, the first night price of the stay will be charged. In case of Non-Refundable or any offers attached to the booking, the full price of the whole stay will be charged on the date of the booking and it will not be refunded and modified in any case.
